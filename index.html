How to play

  1. You get 5 rounds. Each round, an NBA team is drawn.
  2. Pick any all-time player who suited up for that franchise.
  3. Choose one of their seasons — its PPG is added to your total.
  4. Goal: land on exactly 100.0 PPG across all 5 picks.
  5. Go over 100 at any point and you BUST — game over instantly.
Mode differences
Casual — PPG shown for every season. Back button available.
Standard — PPG hidden until you pick. No back, no hints. Unlimited plays.
Daily — PPG hidden until after you pick. No back button. Same board every day.
Nightmare — Random team AND random year shown. Type your player. PPG hidden. No back.
